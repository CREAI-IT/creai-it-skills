# ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Frameworks

## What Is an ICP?

An Ideal Customer Profile defines the exact type of company and buyer who gets the most value from your product — and is therefore most likely to buy. Cold outreach without a clear ICP is spam. With a clear ICP, it's a targeted offer to someone who needs what you built.

## ICP Scoring Model

Score prospects on a 100-point scale across three categories:

### Firmographics (40 points)

| Signal | Score | How to Assess |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Company size matches your sweet spot | 0-10 | LinkedIn company page, Crunchbase |
| Industry/vertical alignment | 0-10 | Company website, LinkedIn |
| Revenue range matches | 0-10 | Crunchbase, news, employee count proxy |
| Geography matches | 0-5 | Company website, LinkedIn |
| Tech stack compatibility | 0-5 | BuiltWith, Wappalyzer, job postings |

### Buyer Persona (30 points)

| Signal | Score | How to Assess |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Job title matches target persona | 0-10 | LinkedIn |
| Decision-making authority | 0-10 | Title seniority, org structure |
| Active on professional channels | 0-5 | LinkedIn activity, Twitter, conferences |
| Content engagement signals | 0-5 | Posts, comments, shares related to your space |

### Intent Signals (30 points)

| Signal | Score | How to Assess |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Recent trigger event (funding, hire, launch) | 0-10 | Crunchbase, LinkedIn, Google News |
| Job postings in relevant area | 0-10 | LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, company careers |
| Technology adoption signals | 0-5 | Job postings mentioning your category |
| Competitor usage | 0-5 | BuiltWith, G2 reviews, job postings |

### Tier Classification

| Tier | Score | Action |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Tier A** | 80-100 | Highest priority. Full personalization. Multi-channel. |
| **Tier B** | 50-79 | Include in sequences. Standard personalization. |
| **Tier C** | Below 50 | Deprioritize or skip. Don't waste personalization effort. |

## Solo Founder Quick ICP Template

If you don't have 50+ closed deals to analyze, use this shortcut:

### 5 Questions to Define Your ICP

1. **Who has the problem your product solves?**
   - Not "who could use it" — who is ACTIVELY suffering from this problem?
   - What does their day look like without your solution?

2. **Who can afford to pay?**
   - What's their budget range for tools in your category?
   - Is this a "nice to have" or a "must solve" for them?

3. **Who can make the buying decision quickly?**
   - Solo decision-maker > committee decision
   - Smaller companies = faster sales cycles

4. **Where do they hang out online?**
   - LinkedIn, Twitter, Reddit, Slack communities?
   - This determines your outreach channels.

5. **What trigger events make them ready to buy?**
   - New hire, funding, product launch, competitor failure?
   - These are your personalization hooks.

### ICP Statement Template

Fill in the blanks:

```
My ideal customer is a [JOB TITLE] at a [COMPANY SIZE]
[INDUSTRY] company, who is struggling with [SPECIFIC PROBLEM].
They currently [CURRENT WORKAROUND], which costs them
[TIME/MONEY/PAIN]. They are most ready to buy when
[TRIGGER EVENT] happens. They can be found on [CHANNELS].
```

**Example:**
```
My ideal customer is a Head of Engineering at a 50-200 person
B2B SaaS company, who is struggling with slow CI/CD pipelines.
They currently use Jenkins with custom scripts, which costs them
2-3 hours/day in developer wait time. They are most ready to buy
when they post a DevOps engineer job listing. They can be found
on LinkedIn and Hacker News.
```

## Negative ICP (Who to Exclude)

Equally important — define who is NOT your customer:

| Exclude If | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Company too small (< X employees) | Can't afford your pricing |
| Company too large (> Y employees) | Sales cycle too long for your stage |
| Wrong industry | Problem doesn't apply |
| No decision-making authority | Will waste your time |
| Already using a deep competitor integration | Switching cost too high |
| No trigger event + no active pain | Cold cold — very low reply rate |

## ICP Validation

After your first 20-30 outreach emails:

| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Reply rate > 10% | ICP is on target |
| Reply rate 5-10% | ICP is close, refine personalization |
| Reply rate < 5% | ICP needs revision — wrong persona or offer |
| Positive replies from unexpected segments | Expand ICP to include them |
| "Not relevant" replies from target segment | Narrow ICP or adjust messaging |
