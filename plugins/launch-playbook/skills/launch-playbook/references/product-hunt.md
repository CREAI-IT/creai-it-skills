# Product Hunt Launch Playbook

## Algorithm & Ranking

Product Hunt ranks products by a weighted combination of:
- **Upvotes** — weighted by voter account age, activity, and voting patterns. New/inactive accounts count less.
- **Comments** — quality and quantity of genuine discussion. Engagement signals are heavily weighted.
- **Engagement velocity** — how quickly upvotes and comments accumulate in the first 4 hours.
- **Anti-gaming** — PH actively detects and penalizes coordinated voting. Never ask people to "upvote" — ask them to "check it out and leave feedback."

Products launch at **12:01 AM PT** and compete for 24 hours. The #1 daily product gets maximum exposure on homepage, newsletter, and social.

### Best Launch Days
- **Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday** — highest traffic, most competition, most exposure.
- **Monday** — good traffic, slightly less competition.
- **Weekend** — low competition but low traffic. Only if you want an easy #1 badge for a smaller audience.

**Recommendation**: Tuesday or Wednesday for maximum exposure. Thursday if you want slightly less competition with still-good traffic.

## Listing Optimization

### Tagline (60 characters max)
The tagline is the single most important copy element. It appears everywhere: homepage feed, emails, social shares.

**Formula**: `[Verb] + [Specific Outcome] + [For Whom / Without Pain]`

**Good examples**:
- "Ship landing pages 10x faster with AI" (outcome + speed)
- "Turn customer feedback into product roadmaps" (transformation)
- "The open-source alternative to Notion" (positioning)

**Bad examples**:
- "The best project management tool" (vague, no differentiator)
- "AI-powered SaaS platform" (meaningless buzzwords)
- "Making the world a better place" (no product information)

**Rules**:
- Lead with a verb (Ship, Build, Turn, Create, Launch)
- Include ONE specific benefit, not three
- Name the category or competitor only if it instantly clarifies
- No emojis in the tagline (save for description)

### Description (260 characters max)
Expand the tagline with mechanism + social proof + CTA.

**Template**:
```
[Product name] [what it does — mechanism]. [Key differentiator or social proof]. [Soft CTA or outcome statement].
```

**Example**:
```
Acme helps indie founders build SEO-optimized landing pages in minutes using AI. No coding needed. Join 2,400+ makers who shipped faster.
```

### Gallery Images (1270x760px, up to 6)
The gallery is your product demo. Treat it like a pitch deck.

**Recommended sequence**:
1. **Hero** — Product screenshot with tagline overlay. Clear, bold, readable at small size.
2. **Problem** — "Before [product]..." — show the pain visually.
3. **Solution** — "With [product]..." — show the transformation.
4. **Key Features** — 3-4 features, each with one-line benefit (not description).
5. **Social Proof** — Testimonial quotes, metrics, or logo bar.
6. **CTA** — Final image with call to action and URL.

**Design rules**:
- Text must be readable at 50% zoom (people scroll fast)
- Consistent brand colors across all images
- Dark backgrounds tend to stand out in the PH feed
- Use actual product screenshots, not illustrations
- GIFs/videos autoplay — use them for image 1 or 2 if possible

### First Comment (Maker Comment)
The first comment sets the tone for all discussion. Post immediately at launch (12:01 AM PT).

**Structure**:
```
Hey PH! 👋

[Personal story — why you built this. 2-3 sentences max. Be specific and authentic.]

[What the product does — one paragraph, benefit-focused.]

[What makes it different — your unique angle, 1-2 sentences.]

[Special offer for PH community — discount, extended trial, etc.]

[Ask for specific feedback — "I'd love to hear what you think about X" or "What features would you want to see next?"]

Happy to answer any questions! 🙏
```

**Rules**:
- Write in first person, be personal and genuine
- Mention the backstory (weekend project, scratch-your-own-itch, pivot story)
- Include a PH-exclusive offer (20% off, extended trial, lifetime deal)
- Ask a specific question to encourage comments
- Keep it under 200 words
- Don't be salesy — be a maker sharing their creation

## Pre-Launch Checklist (T-30 to T-1)

### T-30 to T-21: Foundation
- [ ] Create Product Hunt maker profile (if new)
- [ ] Connect Twitter/X account to PH profile
- [ ] Study top 5 launches in your category from the last 3 months
- [ ] Identify and contact potential hunters (top hunters list on PH)
- [ ] Start building an email list of potential supporters (goal: 100+ people)
- [ ] Join relevant PH communities and engage genuinely (don't spam)

### T-20 to T-11: Asset Preparation
- [ ] Write tagline (test 5 variations, pick the clearest)
- [ ] Write description (260 chars, benefit-driven)
- [ ] Create 5-6 gallery images (1270x760px)
- [ ] Write first comment (maker comment)
- [ ] Prepare social media posts (Twitter thread, LinkedIn post)
- [ ] Create a "launching soon" page on Product Hunt
- [ ] Prepare email to supporters (send T-1)

### T-10 to T-4: Supporter Recruitment
- [ ] Send personal DMs to 50-100 people who would genuinely use your product
- [ ] Post "launching soon" on Twitter/X with product teaser
- [ ] Share in relevant Slack/Discord communities (with value, not spam)
- [ ] Confirm hunter if using one (they need to schedule the post)
- [ ] Test all links, signup flow, and onboarding

### T-3 to T-1: Final Prep
- [ ] Finalize all PH listing details
- [ ] Schedule social media posts
- [ ] Send "launching tomorrow" email to supporters
- [ ] Prepare comment response templates
- [ ] Set up analytics to track PH referral traffic
- [ ] Plan launch day schedule (who does what, when)

## Launch Day Hour-by-Hour

All times in **Pacific Time (PT)**.

| Time (PT) | Action |
|---|---|
| 12:00 AM | Product goes live. Post maker comment immediately. |
| 12:01-12:15 AM | Share link with inner circle (co-founders, close friends, team). |
| 12:15-1:00 AM | Monitor for early comments. Respond to every single one. |
| 5:00-6:00 AM | Send launch email to supporters. "We're live on Product Hunt!" |
| 6:00-7:00 AM | Post Twitter/X launch thread. Post LinkedIn announcement. |
| 7:00-9:00 AM | Peak PH traffic begins. Engage actively with comments. |
| 9:00 AM-12:00 PM | Share in Slack/Discord communities. Cross-post on Reddit (if appropriate). |
| 12:00-2:00 PM | Second wave of engagement. Share user reactions/testimonials on Twitter. |
| 2:00-5:00 PM | Continue responding to comments. Share progress updates on social. |
| 5:00-8:00 PM | Final push. Thank supporters publicly. Share milestone updates. |
| 9:00 PM | Wind down. Prepare "thank you" post for tomorrow. |

### Critical Rules for Launch Day
- **Respond to EVERY comment** within 30 minutes. PH rewards active makers.
- **Never ask for upvotes.** Say "check it out" or "would love your feedback."
- **Share on social with your PH link**, not your product URL.
- **Don't spam communities.** Only share where genuinely relevant.
- **Have at least 2 people** managing the launch (one on PH, one on social).

## Post-Launch (T+1 to T+7)

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| T+1 | Thank voters and commenters individually. Share results on social. Send follow-up email with launch results. |
| T+2 | Engage with remaining comments. Publish a "lessons learned" thread on Twitter. |
| T+3-T+4 | Write a launch retrospective blog post. Share on Indie Hackers, HN. |
| T+5-T+7 | Nurture new signups. Follow up with press contacts. Plan next Product Hunt launch (monthly launches are OK). |

## Metrics & Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Good | Great |
|---|---|---|---|
| Upvotes (24h) | 100-200 | 300-500 | 700+ |
| Comments | 20-40 | 50-100 | 150+ |
| Website visits from PH | 500-1K | 2K-5K | 10K+ |
| Signups from PH day | 50-100 | 200-500 | 1K+ |
| Top 5 finish | ~25% of launches | Top 3 | #1 of the day |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Launching without pre-recruitment | Recruit 50-100 genuine supporters minimum |
| Asking for upvotes directly | Ask for "feedback" or say "check it out" |
| Posting maker comment hours late | Post within 60 seconds of going live |
| Using vague tagline ("AI platform") | Use specific outcome ("Ship landing pages 10x faster") |
| Ignoring comments | Respond to every comment within 30 minutes |
| Launching on Friday/weekend | Launch Tuesday-Thursday for maximum exposure |
| No PH-exclusive offer | Give PH community something special (discount, early access) |
| Launching a half-baked product | Core value must work flawlessly on launch day |
| Not tracking PH referral traffic | Set up UTM parameters and analytics before launch |
