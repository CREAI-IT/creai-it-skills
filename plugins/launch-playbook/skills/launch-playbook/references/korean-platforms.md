# Korean Platform Launch Strategy (한국 시장 런칭 전략)

## Market Context

Korea has a distinct digital ecosystem. Google is not the default search engine — Naver dominates. Kakao owns messaging and payments. Community-driven platforms (cafes, forums) carry more weight than individual social media posts. Mobile-first is not optional — 95%+ of Korean internet users access via mobile.

**Key differences from US/global launches:**
- Search = Naver first, Google second
- Messaging = KakaoTalk (95% penetration)
- Blog content = Naver Blog > WordPress/Medium
- Community = Naver Cafe > Reddit/Discord
- Reviews = 체험단 (experience groups) > individual reviews
- Trust signals = Company registration, 사업자등록번호 (business license number)
- Payment = KakaoPay, Toss, 무통장입금 (bank transfer) alongside card

## Naver

### Naver Blog (네이버 블로그)

Naver Blog is the #1 content platform for Korean product discovery. When Koreans search for product reviews, comparisons, or how-tos, Naver Blog results dominate.

**Writing pattern for Naver Blog SEO:**

```
제목: [키워드] + [구체적 결과/혜택] — 60자 이내
      예: "프로젝트 관리 툴 추천 | 하루 2시간 절약한 방법"

본문 구성:
1. 도입 (2-3줄): 공감 + 문제 제기
2. 본문: 소제목(H2) + 이미지 + 설명 (소제목당 200자 이내)
3. 핵심 정보: 기능, 가격, 사용법 — 구체적 숫자 포함
4. 실제 사용 후기: 스크린샷 + 개인 경험
5. 결론: 추천 대상 + CTA (링크)
```

**Naver Blog SEO rules:**
- **Title**: Include primary keyword naturally. 40-60 characters.
- **Content length**: 1,500-3,000 characters (shorter than English blog posts).
- **Images**: Minimum 5-7 images. Naver weights image-rich posts higher.
- **Tags**: Use 5-10 relevant tags (태그). Include both broad and specific terms.
- **Posting frequency**: Regular posting (2-3x/week) builds blog authority ("최적화 블로그").
- **No external links in body**: Naver deprioritizes posts with external links. Put links in comments or use shortened URLs.
- **Mobile format**: Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max). Large images. Easy to scroll.

**Blog post types for launch:**
1. **제품 리뷰** (Product review) — Detailed walkthrough with screenshots
2. **비교 분석** (Comparison) — Your product vs. alternatives
3. **사용 가이드** (How-to guide) — Step-by-step tutorial
4. **출시 소식** (Launch announcement) — News-style announcement

### Naver Cafe (네이버 카페)

Naver Cafes are topic-specific communities (like subreddits). They have strict self-promotion rules but are powerful for authentic engagement.

**Strategy:**
1. **Join relevant cafes** 2-4 weeks before launch (don't join and immediately post)
2. **Contribute genuinely** — answer questions, share knowledge, be helpful
3. **Post review-style content** — frame as "사용해봤는데" (I tried it) not "광고" (advertisement)
4. **Follow each cafe's rules** — many require minimum post count or membership duration before promotional posts
5. **Engage with responses** — reply to every comment

**High-value cafe categories:**
- IT/개발 (IT/Development) — for developer tools
- 창업/사업 (Startup/Business) — for B2B or productivity tools
- 재테크/경제 (Finance/Economy) — for fintech
- 디자인 (Design) — for design tools
- 마케팅 (Marketing) — for marketing tools

### Naver Smart Store (네이버 스마트스토어)

If your product has a purchase component (SaaS subscription, digital product), consider a Naver Smart Store listing.

**Benefits:**
- Appears in Naver Shopping search results
- Korean consumers trust Naver payment infrastructure
- Built-in review system
- Naver Pay integration (significant conversion boost)

## Kakao

### KakaoTalk Channel (카카오톡 채널)

KakaoTalk Channel (formerly "Plus Friend") is a business messaging account. 95% of Koreans use KakaoTalk daily.

**Launch strategy:**
1. **Create a KakaoTalk Channel** before launch
2. **Build subscribers** through your landing page and Naver Blog posts
3. **Send launch announcement** via channel message (broadcast)
4. **Use rich messages**: image card + button + link

**Message template:**
```
🚀 [제품명] 정식 출시!

[한 줄 설명: 어떤 문제를 어떻게 해결하는지]

✅ [혜택 1]
✅ [혜택 2]
✅ [혜택 3]

🎁 오픈 기념 특별 혜택
[구체적 할인/보너스 내용]

[기한: ~MM/DD까지]

👉 지금 확인하기
[버튼: 링크]
```

**Rules:**
- Keep messages short (mobile screen = small)
- Use emoji sparingly but strategically
- Include a clear deadline for urgency
- Don't send more than 1-2 messages per week (users will block)
- Best send time: 10-11 AM or 7-8 PM (weekdays)

### Kakao Brunch (카카오 브런치)

Brunch is Kakao's long-form content platform (similar to Medium but curated). Good for founder stories and thought leadership.

**Best content types for launch:**
- 창업 스토리 (Founder story) — "왜 이 제품을 만들었는가"
- 문제 인식 에세이 (Problem essay) — Deep dive into the problem you solve
- 개발 일지 (Development log) — Behind the scenes of building

**Writing style:**
- More literary and personal than Naver Blog
- Longer form (2,000-5,000 characters)
- Less keyword-focused, more story-driven
- Minimal images (1-3, quality over quantity)

## 체험단 (Experience Groups)

체험단 is a uniquely Korean marketing system where selected users receive a product for free in exchange for honest reviews on their blogs/social media.

**How it works:**
1. **Create a campaign** on a 체험단 platform
2. **Select participants** based on their blog authority and follower count
3. **Send product/access** to selected participants
4. **Participants create content** (Naver Blog reviews, Instagram posts, etc.)
5. **Content drives organic search traffic** and social proof

**Major 체험단 platforms:**
- 레뷰 (Revu) — largest platform
- 미블 (Mible) — strong for lifestyle/tech
- 위블 (Wible) — growing platform
- 오마이블로그 (OhMyBlog) — blog-focused

**Campaign structure:**
```
캠페인 제목: [제품명] 무료 체험 리뷰어 모집
제공 내용: [무료 이용권/구독/제품]
모집 인원: 10-30명
리뷰 조건: 네이버 블로그 리뷰 1건 (사진 5장 이상, 1,500자 이상)
신청 기간: 7-14일
활동 기간: 리뷰 작성까지 14-21일
```

**Budget**: 체험단 platforms charge per campaign (₩100,000-500,000) + product cost for each participant.

**Tip**: For SaaS/digital products, offer extended free trials or lifetime deals instead of monetary compensation.

## Korean Copywriting Patterns

### Sentence Structure

Korean marketing copy follows different patterns than English:

**Title patterns:**
- `[키워드] 추천 | [구체적 혜택]` — "프로젝트 관리 툴 추천 | 팀 생산성 200% 높이기"
- `[타겟]을 위한 [제품] 완벽 가이드` — "1인 창업자를 위한 랜딩페이지 완벽 가이드"
- `[숫자]가지 [키워드] [동사]` — "5가지 업무 자동화 방법 총정리"

**CTA patterns:**
- `지금 시작하기` (Start now)
- `무료로 체험하기` (Try for free)
- `자세히 알아보기` (Learn more)
- `바로 확인하기` (Check it out now)
- `무료 상담 신청` (Request free consultation)

**Trust-building phrases:**
- `00개 기업이 선택한` (Chosen by X companies)
- `누적 사용자 00명 돌파` (X cumulative users surpassed)
- `전문가가 추천하는` (Recommended by experts)
- `실제 사용 후기` (Real user reviews)

### Tone Differences

| Context | Korean Approach |
|---|---|
| Formal B2B | 합니다/습니다 endings (존댓말). Professional but warm. |
| Casual B2C | 해요/이에요 endings. Friendly, approachable. |
| Community post | 했어요/해봤는데 endings. Conversational, peer-to-peer. |
| Ad copy | Short, punchy. 3-5 word phrases. |

**Key rule**: Korean copy is generally shorter and more direct than English copy. Long paragraphs don't work on Korean platforms (mobile-first reading behavior).

### Meta descriptions (Korean)
- Length: 80-120 characters (shorter than English)
- Include primary keyword
- End with benefit or CTA

## Timing & Scheduling (Korean Market)

### Best posting times
- **Naver Blog**: 9-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 8-9 PM (KST)
- **KakaoTalk Channel**: 10-11 AM, 7-8 PM (KST)
- **Naver Cafe**: 10 AM-12 PM, 2-4 PM (KST)
- **Kakao Brunch**: 8-9 PM (KST, leisure reading time)

### Best launch days
- **Monday-Thursday** for B2B
- **Thursday-Friday** for B2C (weekend spending mindset)
- **Avoid**: Major Korean holidays (설날, 추석), exam periods (수능 season)

### Korean launch timeline adaptation

| Period | Korean-specific actions |
|---|---|
| T-30 | Create Naver Blog and KakaoTalk Channel. Register 체험단 campaign. |
| T-21 | Start posting Naver Blog content (3-4 posts for SEO indexing). |
| T-14 | 체험단 recruitment opens. Join relevant Naver Cafes. |
| T-7 | 체험단 selected, products/access sent. Pre-launch KakaoTalk message. |
| T-1 | Final Naver Blog post: "내일 출시" announcement. |
| Launch Day | KakaoTalk broadcast. Naver Cafe posts. Social media (Instagram/Twitter KR). |
| T+1 to T+7 | 체험단 reviews start publishing. Share and amplify reviews. |
| T+14 to T+21 | All 체험단 reviews live. Aggregate and share best quotes. |

## Mobile-First Design for Korean Audience

Korean users are overwhelmingly mobile. All content must be mobile-optimized:

- **Paragraph length**: 2-3 lines max before line break
- **Image width**: Full-width on mobile (no side-by-side layouts)
- **Font size**: Minimum 16px for body text
- **CTA buttons**: Large, thumb-friendly (minimum 48x48px)
- **Page load**: Under 3 seconds on 4G/LTE
- **Payment**: Support KakaoPay, Toss, Naver Pay alongside international cards
- **Signup**: Support Kakao/Naver social login (higher conversion than email in Korea)
