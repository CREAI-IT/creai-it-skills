# Competitor Intelligence Module

Use when the case requires competitor mapping, whitespace analysis, strategic positioning, or response strategy.

## Competitor Taxonomy (MECE)

1. Direct competitors: same customer + same core use case.
2. Adjacent competitors: overlapping problem, different product shape.
3. Substitutes: non-software or workflow alternatives solving the same outcome.

## Analysis Dimensions

1. Segment focus and ICP.
2. Product scope and differentiators.
3. Pricing and packaging structure.
4. Distribution channels and demand capture.
5. Proof points (customers, growth signals, partnerships).
6. Defensibility (data, workflow lock-in, ecosystem, brand).

## Output Requirement

Return:
1. Top 3 threats with evidence.
2. Top 3 whitespace opportunities.
3. Positioning statement recommendation.

## Common Mistakes

1. Treating all competitors as direct.
2. Describing features without GTM implications.
3. Ignoring substitutes and incumbent distribution power.
