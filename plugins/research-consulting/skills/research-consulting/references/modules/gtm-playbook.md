# GTM Playbook Module

Use when the case requires market-entry strategy, channel prioritization, sales motion design, or first-100-customer planning.

## Core Questions

1. Who is the priority ICP and buying committee?
2. What problem urgency and willingness-to-pay exist?
3. Which channels can acquire customers fastest at acceptable cost?
4. Which sales motion fits price point and procurement friction?

## Framework

1. Define ICP using firmographic + behavioral + intent signals.
2. Map JTBD and pain severity by segment.
3. Build channel options (owned, earned, paid, partner, outbound).
4. Score channels by speed, reach, intent quality, and execution load.
5. Select motion (self-serve, low-touch, sales-led, hybrid).

## Required Tables

### Channel Scorecard
| Channel | Reach | Intent quality | CAC efficiency | Time to first wins | Operational complexity | Weighted score |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|

### Motion Fit
| Motion | Best ACV range | Cycle length | Team requirements | Main risk |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|

## Output Requirement

Return:
1. Top 2 channels to prioritize now.
2. Top 1 channel to defer and reason.
3. 30/60/90 experiment plan with KPIs.

## Common Mistakes

1. Recommending channels without reachability evidence.
2. Ignoring cycle length mismatch with price point.
3. Mixing ICP hypotheses and validated segment evidence.
