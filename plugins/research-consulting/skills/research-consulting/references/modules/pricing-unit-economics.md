# Pricing and Unit Economics Module

Use when the case requires pricing decisions, packaging strategy, conversion economics, or revenue scenario modeling.

## Core Questions

1. What willingness-to-pay signal exists by segment?
2. Which packaging tier structure maps to buyer maturity?
3. What unit economics support sustainable growth?
4. Which levers have highest impact on revenue and margin?

## Core Metrics and Formulas

```text
Paid conversions = leads × conversion rate
New MRR = new customers × monthly price
MRR_t = MRR_(t-1) × (1 - churn) + new MRR
LTV (simple) = ARPA × gross margin / churn
Payback period = CAC / monthly gross profit per customer
```

## Common Mistakes

1. Pricing recommendation without benchmark anchors.
2. Ignoring churn and attach-rate sensitivity.
3. Mixing gross and net revenue assumptions.
