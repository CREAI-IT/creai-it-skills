# Customer Segmentation Module

Use when the case requires ICP definition, persona prioritization, or segment-level GTM focus.

## Segmentation Axes

1. Firmographic: industry, size, geography, maturity.
2. Behavioral: current workflow, tool adoption, buying behavior.
3. Needs-based: JTBD, pain frequency, pain severity.
4. Economic: budget authority, willingness-to-pay, expected LTV.

## Prioritization Framework

Score each segment on:
1. Pain severity.
2. Budget authority.
3. Reachability.
4. Win probability.
5. Revenue potential.

## Output Requirement

Return:
1. Priority segment ranking.
2. Why top segment wins now.
3. Risks if segment assumptions are wrong.

## Common Mistakes

1. Segmenting by industry only without behavior or economics.
2. Ignoring who actually has spending authority.
3. Choosing broad TAM segment that is hard to reach quickly.
