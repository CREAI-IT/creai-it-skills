# Short Video — YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels

## Platform Overview

Short-form video is the fastest-growing content format. All three platforms (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels) share similar mechanics but have distinct cultures and algorithms.

### Cross-Platform Specs

| Spec | YouTube Shorts | TikTok | Instagram Reels |
|------|---------------|--------|-----------------|
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 (vertical) | 9:16 (vertical) | 9:16 (vertical) |
| Resolution | 1080x1920px | 1080x1920px | 1080x1920px |
| Duration | 15-60s | 15s-10min | 15-90s |
| Sweet spot | 30-45s | 15-60s | 15-30s |
| Captions | Auto-generated, editable | Auto-generated, styles | Auto-generated |
| Music | YouTube library | Full music library | Instagram/Meta library |
| Text on screen | Recommended | Essential | Recommended |
| Hashtags | 3-5 in description | 3-5 in description | 3-5 in caption |

### Key Differences

| Aspect | YouTube Shorts | TikTok | Reels |
|--------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Audience | Broader, all ages | Younger, trend-driven | Instagram's existing audience |
| Discovery | Shorts shelf + search | For You Page (FYP) | Reels tab + Explore |
| Monetization | Shorts Fund + ads | Creator Fund + gifts | Bonuses (limited) |
| Virality ceiling | Very high (YouTube scale) | Highest (global FYP) | Moderate |
| B2B viability | Growing | Low | Moderate |
| Evergreen potential | High (YouTube search) | Low (trend-driven) | Low |
| Cross-post from | Original or TikTok | Original | Original or TikTok |
| Watermarks | No TikTok watermarks | — | No TikTok watermarks |

## Script Framework

### The Hook-Body-CTA Structure

```
HOOK (0-3 seconds):
  — Pattern interrupt that stops the scroll
  — Must work without audio (many watch on mute)
  — Text overlay is critical

  Hook types:
  - Question: "Did you know [surprising fact]?"
  - Bold claim: "[Contrarian statement that sounds wrong]"
  - Before/After: Show the transformation immediately
  - Pain point: "Stop doing [common mistake]"
  - Curiosity: "The one thing [experts] never tell you about [topic]"
  - Number: "[N] ways to [outcome] you haven't tried"

BODY (3-45 seconds):
  — Deliver on the hook's promise immediately
  — One idea only. Don't try to cover multiple points.
  — Use text overlays to reinforce key points
  — Keep energy consistent (don't start high and drop)

  Body structures:
  A. Step-by-step: "Step 1... Step 2... Step 3..."
  B. Problem-solution: "The problem... Here's the fix..."
  C. Story arc: "This happened... Then this... The result..."
  D. List reveal: "#3... #2... #1..."
  E. Demo: Show the thing working in real-time

CTA (last 3-5 seconds):
  — Soft, not pushy
  — "Follow for more [topic]"
  — "Save this for later"
  — "Comment [word] and I'll send you [resource]"
  — "Part 2 is on my profile"
  — NEVER: "Like and subscribe!" (cringe, ignored)
```

### Script Templates


## Platform-Specific Notes

### YouTube Shorts

- **Title matters**: YouTube Shorts have a title field — use it for SEO
- **Description**: Include keywords (YouTube Shorts appear in YouTube search)
- **First frame**: Must be compelling (it's the thumbnail on the Shorts shelf)
- **Loops**: YouTube rewards videos that loop (viewer watches again)
- **No watermarks**: Remove TikTok watermarks before uploading
- **Posting time**: 2-4pm ET weekdays, 9-11am ET weekends
- **Frequency**: 3-7 Shorts per week for growth, 1-2 for maintenance

### TikTok

- **Trend-aware**: Use trending sounds and formats (but adapt, don't copy)
- **First second**: If you lose them in 1 second, algorithm kills distribution
- **Completion rate**: The #1 metric. Shorter = higher completion = more distribution
- **Comments drive distribution**: Ask questions that invite responses
- **Stitch/Duet**: Reacting to trending content extends your reach
- **Posting time**: 7-9am, 12-3pm, or 7-11pm (varies by niche)
- **Frequency**: 1-3x daily for growth, 3-5x weekly for maintenance
- **No external links**: Use "link in bio" — TikTok penalizes outbound links

### Instagram Reels

- **Cover image**: Choose a custom cover that looks good on your grid
- **Caption**: Write a full caption (not just hashtags) — captions drive saves
- **Audio**: Use trending audio when possible (boosts distribution)
- **Grid aesthetic**: Reels should visually fit your Instagram grid
- **Posting time**: 9am-12pm weekdays, 6-9pm evenings
- **Frequency**: 3-5 Reels per week for growth

## Video Content Quality Rules

- **Text overlays are mandatory** — 85% of social video is watched on mute
- **Captions/subtitles always on** — accessibility + comprehension
- **One idea per video** — don't try to teach a course in 60 seconds
- **Energy must match platform** — TikTok is energetic, YouTube is more measured
- **Don't start with "Hey guys"** — instant scroll. Hook first, intro never.
- **Vertical only** — horizontal video cropped to vertical looks amateur
- **No long intros** — value in the first 3 seconds or lose them
- **End with a reason to follow** — tease future content or series
